tripoli, libya( ap) u.n. secretary-general kofi annan arrived in libya saturday for talks aimed at bringing to trial two libyan suspects in the 1988 pan am bombing over lockerbie, scotland.
on saturday, u.n. secretary-general kofi annan met with gadhafi to iron out the details.
annan flew into libya aboard a special plane from jerba after receiving clearance from the u.n. sanctions committee.
qatar's foreign minister, sheik hamad bin jassem bin jaber al thani, met libyan leader moammar gadhafi in the desert on saturday, libya's official television reported.
