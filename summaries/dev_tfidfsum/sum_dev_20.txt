thai newspapers reported saturday that attempts to convince saudi arabia to participate in the upcoming 13th asian games have failed.
the asian games will be held from the dec. 6-20.
a traditional thai massage parlor has been opened at the games village.
the bangkok games are expected to attract more than 10,000 athletes from 43 nations.
saudi sports authorities said the games conflicted with national centenary celebrations and the muslim holy month of ramadan.
thai olympic committee chairman gen. chettha thanajaro told the bangkok post he sent a last-minute plea to his saudi counterpart but has received no response.
